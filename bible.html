Bible Reading Program

The Timazi Bible Reading Program is designed to help teenagers, young adults and families read the Bible consistently and develop a lifetime habit of daily Bible reading.

Bible Reading Program

If we are going to develop a biblical worldview, then we must read the Bible. Only a few Christians read their Bible from Genesis to Revelation. Most of us have wished, tried or even dreamt accomplishing that task. With the Timazi Bible Reading Program, that will no longer be a wish or a dream. Easy to follow and implement, this Bible Reading program is designed specifically for student as young as 12, teacher trainees as well as parents to use together with their children. It uses the easy-to-read version of the New Living Translation accompanied by a journal with daily reflections split in three manuals for each term.
